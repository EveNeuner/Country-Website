€1,000,000 is thrown into the Trevi Fountain every year

There is a legend that says that you'll return to Rome if
you toss a coin into the Trevi. Not suprisingly, many tourists
toss coins into the fountain, in total about €3,000 every day.
This adds to about €1,000,000 every year, which is all donated.
